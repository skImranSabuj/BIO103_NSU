## 2) Six Classes of Nutrients

| Nutrient | English Explanation | বাংলা ব্যাখ্যা |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Carbohydrates | Main and preferred energy source; starch is broken into glucose. | প্রধান ও পছন্দের শক্তির উৎস; শ্বেতসার ভেঙে গ্লুকোজ হয়। |
| Fats | Provide concentrated energy, build membranes, and store fat-soluble vitamins. | ঘনীভূত শক্তি দেয়, ঝিল্লি তৈরি করে এবং চর্বিতে দ্রবণীয় ভিটামিন সঞ্চয় করে। |
| Proteins | Build and repair tissues; form enzymes and hormones. | টিস্যু গঠন ও মেরামত করে; এনজাইম ও হরমোন তৈরি করে। |
| Vitamins | Help regulate metabolism and support growth and health. | বিপাক নিয়ন্ত্রণে সাহায্য করে এবং বৃদ্ধি ও স্বাস্থ্য রক্ষা করে। |
| Minerals | Needed for structure, fluid balance, and physiological functions. | গঠন, তরল ভারসাম্য ও শারীরবৃত্তীয় কাজের জন্য প্রয়োজন। |
| Water | Medium for reactions, transport, temperature control, and excretion. | বিক্রিয়ার মাধ্যম, পরিবহন, তাপ নিয়ন্ত্রণ ও রেচনের জন্য দরকার। |

## 5) BMI and Healthy Weight

| English | বাংলা |
| --- | --- |
| BMI means Body Mass Index and is used to estimate healthy body weight. | BMI অর্থ বডি মাস ইনডেক্স, যা স্বাস্থ্যকর দেহের ওজন অনুমান করতে ব্যবহৃত হয়। |
| Formula: BMI = Weight (kg) / Height squared (m2) | সূত্র: BMI = ওজন (কেজি) / উচ্চতা² (মিটার²) |
| Normal BMI is generally 18.5 to 24.9. | সাধারণত স্বাভাবিক BMI হলো ১৮.৫ থেকে ২৪.৯। |
| BMI does not directly measure body fat distribution or muscle mass. | BMI সরাসরি দেহের চর্বির বণ্টন বা পেশির পরিমাণ মাপে না। |

### BMI Categories

| Category | BMI Range |
| --- | --- |
| Underweight | Less than 18.5 |
| Normal | 18.5 to 24.9 |
| Overweight | 25 to 29.9 |
| Obese | 30 or more |

## 7) Side Notes, Shortcuts, and Common Mistakes

### High-Yield Tips

- Energy nutrients: carbohydrate, fat, protein.
- Non-energy nutrients: vitamins, minerals, water.
- Fat gives more than double the energy of carbohydrate or protein per gram.
- BMI is a screening tool, not a full body-composition test.

### Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Mistake: "Vitamins give calories." Correct: Vitamins do not provide energy.
- Mistake: "Protein is the preferred energy source." Correct: Protein is mainly for building and repair.
- Mistake: "A normal BMI always means perfect health." Correct: BMI has limitations.
- Mistake: "Malnutrition only means not eating enough." Correct: It includes undernutrition, overnutrition, and imbalance.

## 8) Solved Examples

### Example 1: Calculate BMI for a student weighing 60 kg with height 1.6 m.

- BMI = 60 / (1.6 x 1.6)
- BMI = 60 / 2.56
- BMI = 23.4 approximately

Final answer: BMI is about 23.4, which falls in the normal range.

### Example 2: Which nutrient gives the highest energy per gram?

- Carbohydrate = 4 Cal/g
- Protein = 4 Cal/g
- Fat = 9 Cal/g

Final answer: Fat gives the highest energy per gram.

## 9) MCQ Practice (Lec 21-22)

1. Which nutrient is the body's preferred source of energy?
   A) Protein B) Carbohydrate C) Vitamin C D) Calcium  
   Answer: B  
   Why: Carbohydrates are the main and preferred energy source.

2. Which nutrient provides 9 calories per gram?
   A) Protein B) Vitamin D C) Fat D) Water  
   Answer: C  
   Why: Fat is the most energy-dense nutrient listed.

3. Which of the following does not provide calories?
   A) Fat B) Protein C) Carbohydrate D) Mineral  
   Answer: D  
   Why: Minerals are essential but do not provide energy.

4. BMI is calculated using:
   A) Age and weight B) Height and age C) Weight and height D) Pulse and height  
   Answer: C  
   Why: BMI depends on weight divided by height squared.

5. Which vitamin group is usually not stored much in the body?
   A) A, D, E, K B) B and C C) Only vitamin A D) Only vitamin K  
   Answer: B  
   Why: Water-soluble vitamins are not stored significantly.

6. Undernutrition means:
   A) Too much sugar intake B) Too much exercise C) Too little nutrient intake D) Only vitamin overdose  
   Answer: C  
   Why: Undernutrition means inadequate intake of food or nutrients.

7. Which mineral is most closely linked with hemoglobin formation?
   A) Sodium B) Iron C) Calcium D) Potassium  
   Answer: B  
   Why: Iron is needed to make hemoglobin.

8. Which statement is correct?
   A) Water provides 9 Cal/g
   B) Vitamins are the main fuel source
   C) Protein mainly helps build and repair tissues
   D) BMI directly measures body fat percentage  
   Answer: C  
   Why: Protein is mainly structural and functional, not the preferred fuel source.

## 🧠 Must-Remember One-Liners

- **Biology** = Study of life and living things
- **Metabolism** = Anabolism (build) + Catabolism (break)
- **Cell Theory** = All life is made of cells; cells come from cells
- **Prokaryotic** = No nucleus (bacteria) | **Eukaryotic** = Has nucleus (animals, plants)
- **Mitochondria** = Powerhouse of the cell (makes ATP)
- **Chloroplast** = Solar panel of the cell (photosynthesis)
- **Central Dogma** = DNA → RNA → Protein
- **Aerobic** = 38 ATP (with O₂) | **Anaerobic** = 2 ATP (without O₂)
